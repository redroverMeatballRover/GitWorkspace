• Moving a single piece from a location to another location.
Examples:

o d8 h4 //moves the piece on d8 to h4
o c4 d6* //moves the piece on c4 to d6 and captures the
piece on d6





• Moving two pieces.
Examples:
1. e1 g1 h1 f1 // moves the king from e1 to g1 and the
rook from h1 to f1 (called king-side castling)
